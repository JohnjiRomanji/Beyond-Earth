About Us

Beyond Earth was a company founded to revolutionize space travel. We seek to make space travel easier, safer, and cheaper for all parties involved.

Currently commercial space travel is a luxury for people of wealth and caliber of milionares, but what if it wasn't? What if the average person could travel to space and have an out of this world experience. That is our goal at Beyond Earth.
