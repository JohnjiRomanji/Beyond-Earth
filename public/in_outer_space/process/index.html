In Space

Going to space has historically been a risky endeavour. However, Beyond Earth's unique safety systems and well trained crew will make sure you have the best experience in space and bring you back home, safe and sound.

Takeoff

Our best rockets can deliver 7 million pounds of thrust to carry up to 20,000 pounds of cargo, while still allowing for a secure departure from Earth.

Orbit

Our shuttles allow you to experience the best of outer space while in orbit. Although we have a gravitational system that can simulate the Earth's gravity, we can disable this system and allow you to experience zero gravity, so you can choose how you live comfortably while in orbit.

Landing

The landing processes on the Earth and the Moon are both safe with our unique self-slignment system allowing thrusters to guide you to the ground safely.
